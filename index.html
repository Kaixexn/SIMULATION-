About the Project

Why We Built This

Communication is at the heart of everything we do — from a simple text message to a full multimedia campaign. Yet most of us never formally learn why some messages succeed and others fail.

Our group created CommLab as an interactive way to explore the science and art behind effective communication. Instead of just reading about communication models, you get to run live simulations, make scenario choices, and test your knowledge — all in one place.

This project covers everything from foundational communication theory to practical multimedia strategy — designed specifically for high school learners.

  • 🎯
    Help learners understand key communication models and how messages travel from sender to receiver
  • ⚗️
    Provide interactive simulations that make abstract theory visible and hands-on
  • 🌍
    Explore how cultural differences affect communication and multimedia interpretation
  • 🏆
    Make learning engaging through gamification — XP, badges, and a skill tree

What's Inside CommLab?

  • 📖
    9 Tutorial Lessons
    From communication basics to visual design and language tone — with branching scenario choices
  • ⚗️
    5 Live Simulations
    Noise channel, broken telephone chain, media budget allocator, cultural lens filter, feedback loop
  • 📚
    Searchable Glossary
    22 key terms with category filters — your quick reference for every concept
  • 📊
    Personal Dashboard
    Track your XP, quiz scores, skill tree progress, and earned achievement badges
  • 🎮
    Gamified Learning
    Progressive lesson unlock, XP points, and badges keep you motivated to finish
